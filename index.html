About the club

The SuperNova Research Club was founded in the 2024-2025 school year through the efforts of the student council. The club's purpose is to bring a variety of opportunities in research and higher education to SuperNovians and ensure they have access to high quality guidance and resources to guarantee their future success in the real world in all fields, including higher education, academia and the workforce.

Why do research? Isn't that hard?

While it's true that often times research is not an easy feat, that doesn't make it any less worthwhile as a project! According to the club's own research, the 2/3 Pakistanis who received offers of admission to Harvard University in the 2023-2024 school year all had crowning awards in research, and many more candidates admitted to top schools, including Brown, Columbia, Cornell, Dartmouth, Duke, Princeton, Stanford and others! Don't believe us? Here are some profiles, their school, city, what they researched and any other relevant info!

  • LGS Johar Town-OPF, Lahore, Fractals, Harvard, Princeton & Caltech
  • Cadet College Hasanabdal, Hasanabdal, Particle Physics, Harvard
  • LGS Defence Phase I, Lahore, Waste Recycling, Brown, Columbia & Stanford
  • Kaizen High, Lahore, Financial Literacy, Duke
  • and many others...

Research doesn't have to be boring. It can be fun, fresh, and based on something you love. We're sure there's at least ONE topic you wouldn't mind learning a little more about from what you've studied at school alone, so why not turn it into a proper, passionate extracurricular that might be your potential pathway to a top global school?
